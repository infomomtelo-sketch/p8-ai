How it works

A cleaner operating system for your property

RunP8 follows a service structure that helps owners understand what happens next, why it matters, and how support continues after tenant placement.

Step 1

Start with a clear property intake

Owners begin with a simple intake so the property, goals, address, and service needs are clear from the beginning.

Simple intake with contact details, address, and service selection
Clear direction on whether you need placement, full management, or ongoing support
Step 2

Prepare and position the property correctly

Once submitted, the property moves into a guided preparation flow with pricing support, photography, showings, and inspection readiness.

Professional presentation and better pricing visibility
Home inspection and property condition flow made clearer
Step 3

Place a qualified and reliable tenant

Tenant placement should feel organized, not chaotic. The process is built to make presentation, screening, and lease flow easier to follow.

Showings and presentation handled more professionally
Background checks, income verification, and lease flow done properly
Step 4

Manage the property without constant stress

After placement, owners should still feel supported through maintenance, accounting, inspections, and renewals.

Maintenance requests handled without you coordinating every detail
Renewal, inspection, and operational steps kept inside one cleaner system
