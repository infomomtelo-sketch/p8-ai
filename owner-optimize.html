What we are optimizing now

Before publication, the property should be positioned clearly enough to support stronger renter response and a cleaner listing experience.

Optimization is where pricing, clarity, and presentation are reviewed before the listing becomes publicly visible.
Pricing review Check whether the target rent is positioned realistically for the market and property type.
Listing clarity Review the strength of the property description and how clearly the setup is explained.
Presentation quality Confirm that the listing looks complete enough to support renter trust before going live.
Publication readiness Make sure the property can move into renter-facing listings without missing critical details.
